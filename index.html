My Design Process

In my previous assignment, I felt slightly guilty that I didn't get the time to actually utilize the lightsaber that I prompt a player to choose at the start of the game. Thus, this version of Whack-A-Mole has grown from the want to actually give the user a functioning lightsaber.


For the most part, I stuck to Whack-A-Mole's roots when designing this game. However, the key difference here is that the player is awarded points and misses based on which lightsaber they choose at the very start. If they choose a "Light Side" lightsaber, then they are awarded points for striking dark side users, and misses for strking light side users— vice-versa if the "Dark Side" is chosen. Programming this provided an enjoyable amount of challenge!
